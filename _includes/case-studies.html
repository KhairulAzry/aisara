Case Studies

We've done several sace studies to make sure AiSara is compatible to any kind of situation.

Housing Prices

The surface solution visualises the complex relationship between the price of a house and a number of variables, housing median age and total number of rooms being the two most largely influencing variables.

 

The red dot shows that the most expensive house has 3 rooms and is less than 2 years of age.

Oil and Gas Reservoir Model

The surface solution visualizes the optimized values for the variables (fault threshold and pore volume multiplier, among others) to reduce error in the model.

 

This enables the engineer to produce the most accurate version of the reservoir model.

Shell's Blue Sky Scenario

In this example, we take a look at Shell’s ’Blue Sky’ Scenario, where Shell targets to meet the goal of the Paris Agreement — to hold the increase in global average temperature to well below 2°C.

 

In the visualization, we can see the energy consumption per year (in exajoules) and how it is affected by the variables, GDP and time being the most impactful variables.

 

Reference : Shell Sky Scenario
